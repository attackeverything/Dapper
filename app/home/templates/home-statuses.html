Submission Statuses

When a model has been submitted to the system, there are four statuses of the submission that can be displayed on the view submissions page.

These statuses will be displayed under the "Status" column and include the following:

  • pending - the submission is waiting for the system to begin executing it
  • started - the system has began executing the submission
  • completed - the submission successfully completed and results are now visible
  • failed - the submission failed and results will not be shown
